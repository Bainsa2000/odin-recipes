Beef Curry

Description

Beef curry is a dish that is very popular in Bangladesh.

Ingredients

  • Beef Cubes
  • Onions
  • Butter/Oil
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Paste
  • Vinegar
  • Water
  • Meat Masala Spice Mix

Steps

  1. Chop Onions and Garlic
  2. Add Onions into pan and cook until translucent
  3. Add tomato paste and Meat Masala into the pan
  4. Stir until fragrant or tomato paste loses moisture
  5. Add the Garlic
  6. Mix in the Beef and cook for a few minutes
  7. Add water until all ingredients are submerged
  8. Add 2 lids of Vinegar to reduce spice level
  9. Let the mixture cook for 2 hours until the beef is tender
  10. Remove from heat and enjoy!
