Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • Cubes of Chicken
  • Garam Masala
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Corriander
  • Tomato Paste

Instructions

  • Cut Onions and place into pan with butter
  • When onions become translucent add spices and tomato paste
  • Stir until paste loses moisture and add cubes of chicken
  • Cook chicken for a few minutes and then add water
  • Simmer until most of the water evaporates
  • Add corriander and enjoy!
